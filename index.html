Project Team 14: Demographics of MBTA vs Census, COSI 116A Fall 2024

Lewen Sun, Brian Shen, Jonghyeon Lee, Ajibola Falade

Project-long Course Project as part of COSI 116A: Information Visualization, taught by Prof. Dylan Cashman, Brandeis University.

Visualization

Click and drag over the map to highlight the neighborhoods of Boston. Highlighting neighborhoods will show the census and ridership demographics of the selected neighborhoods. A bar chart of the gated entries in each station in each neighborhood is on the bottom.
